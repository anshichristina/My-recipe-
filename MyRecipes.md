README.md
the recipe is about how to make marshmellow hot choclate with both milk and water.

ingredients.md
1 cup of water/ milk(for one person)
hot choclate powder 
vanilla flavored marshmellows

recipe.md
water hot choclate:
1. boil the water in the microwave for 2 minute
2. add the hot choclate powder into the hot water. 
3. stir the drink for 1 minute straight. 
4. add like 4-6 marshmellows into the hot choclate and enjoy the drink. 

milk hot choclate:
1. boil the milk in the microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds. 
2. follow steps 2-4.
